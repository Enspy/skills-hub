## What Boundless is

**One-liner:** Compliance infrastructure for onchain finance.
**Core promise:** Go onchain without exposing company data.
**Category:** Compliant onchain infrastructure for global finance.

Boundless adds confidentiality, compliance controls, and audit access to financial operations on public blockchain rails. It enables payments, payroll, treasury, and tokenized asset workflows without exposing sensitive transaction data, balances, counterparties, or operational flows to the public market.

Boundless works with existing rails, compliance systems, and operational workflows. No migration required.

---

## The problem Boundless solves

Public blockchains expose everything by default:
- Treasury balances and rebalancing movements
- Counterparty relationships and payment history
- Payroll amounts, cadence, and headcount signals
- Revenue flows and customer wallet activity
- Asset ownership and deal structure

**Sharpest version: Onchain rails expose your company's data.**

---

## Core product: Boundless Shield™

Shield is Boundless's infrastructure product. It delivers three capabilities on any supported blockchain:

1. **Confidential transaction data** — transaction details visible only to authorized parties
2. **Continuous compliance** — KYC/KYB and sanctions screening on every participant and transaction, through your existing compliance providers
3. **Programmable controls** — who can transact, under what limits, who sees what

Use "Shield" after first mention. Use "Boundless Shield™" on first mention in launch copy.

- **Banks and custodians** — cross-border settlement, client asset management

- **Fintechs and neobanks** — onchain financial products with compliance included
- **Asset managers** — DeFi yield access with compliance approval

Our job is NOT selling the benefits of blockchain or stablecoins. The buyer already knows that. The blocker is that public infrastructure exposes too much.

## Compliance providers

Shield integrates with existing compliance providers. Name them specifically:
- **Persona** — KYC/KYB identity verification
- **Chainalysis** — transaction screening
- **TRM Labs** — wallet monitoring and risk scoring

Never say "Shield does KYC" or "Shield verifies users." Always: Shield integrates with existing compliance providers to run KYC/KYB and sanctions screening.

## Vocabulary rules

### Always use
- **"confidentiality"** — not "privacy" (noun)
- **"onchain"** — one word, lowercase (not "on-chain")
- **"compliance framework"** — not "mandate"
- **"compliance providers"** — when referring to Persona, Chainalysis, TRM Labs collectively
- **"programmable controls"** — not "policy engine" in marketing copy

### Never use
- **"privacy"** as a noun — use "confidentiality" instead
- **"anonymity"** — never
- **"institutional-grade"** — commodity phrase, avoid leading with it
- **"pools"** — not in Boundless vocabulary. Exception: "privacy pools" is acceptable when naming a competitor category. Never use "pools" to describe anything Boundless does.
- **"mandate"** — use "compliance framework" or "compliance team"
- **"layer"** — avoid as a product descriptor
- **"seamless," "powerful," "robust," "cutting-edge," "innovative"** — replace with specific outcomes
- **"DeFi-native," "permissionless," "trustless," "web3"** — too crypto-native for institutional audience

## Three-pillar order

Always: **Confidentiality. Compliance. Control.**

Never invert. The logic: benefit (what you get) → enabler (how it works) → control plane (what you manage).
